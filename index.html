Upload Your Files


To send your audio files to SoundMaster Studios, read the instructions below and then click the "Upload Now" button. Do let us know if you have any questions. Thanks!

Instructions For Uploading

  1. Place all of the files you wish to upload into a folder on your computer. Please also include a text document containing your notes and instructions in the same folder.


  2. When you are ready, click the "Upload Now" button below. This will open a new page in your browser at pCloud, our file handling service.


  3. Once you are on the pCloud page, select all of the files you would like to upload (including the document with your instructions), and drag them onto the screen.


  4. Enter your name AND email address into the field labeled "Your name". There is no separate field for email address.


  5. Click the "UPLOAD" button and wait for the confirmation message: "Files are uploaded!" NOTE: It is not necessary to add a password or create an account after uploading. That is merely an option provided by the PCloud service.
